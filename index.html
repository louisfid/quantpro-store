Frequently Asked Questions
Does the free trial really cost nothing? ›
Yes. Your first month of Pro is completely free. We require a credit card to start your trial so renewal is seamless — you won't be charged until the trial ends.
Can I cancel before the trial ends? ›
Absolutely. Cancel any time during the trial and you won't be charged. Your Pro access continues until the trial period is over.
What happens if I don't upgrade after the trial? ›
Your account automatically downgrades to the free Starter plan. You keep full access to Dashboard, Depot, Analyse, Schnellanalyse, and Settings.
Is the annual plan billed all at once? ›
Yes — the annual plan (€96/year) is billed once upfront and saves you 20% compared to monthly billing.
Are prices inclusive of VAT? ›
Prices shown are exclusive of VAT. German customers will see 19% VAT added at checkout.
